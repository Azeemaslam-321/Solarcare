Best for homes

When the ₹499 plan is a good fit

This plan works well when the rooftop is small, access is straightforward, and the customer mainly needs a cleaning visit to remove dust and visible buildup.

Best value

When premium maintenance makes more sense

If the site needs cleaning plus inspection, output-related discussion, or recurring upkeep planning, the premium plan usually gives better value than a basic one-off visit.

Helpful pricing advice

Compare what is included, not only the number.

A low entry price is useful, but customers should also check whether the visit includes only cleaning, whether site condition will be reviewed, and whether follow-up support is available if the rooftop needs more care later.
