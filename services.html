Core support

Best for rooftops with visible dust, bird droppings, sticky dirt, or long gaps since the last cleaning visit.

Maintenance visit

Useful when output has dropped, the inverter is showing an issue, or the customer wants a site-specific discussion before booking more work.

Bird netting support

Recommended when pigeons or birds keep creating repeated dirt, nesting, or under-panel access problems.

Battery and streetlight support

Handled where applicable for battery health checks, replacement planning, and related solar streetlight requirements.

AMC and repeat care

Best for larger rooftops, institutions, and commercial sites that need regular service intervals instead of one-off cleaning.
