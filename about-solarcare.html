Why maintenance matters

Cleaning is only one part of good rooftop care.

Solar maintenance is not just about appearance. A regular visit can also help reveal repeated dirt patterns, bird-related damage, blocked roof areas, visible wiring concerns, or access issues that may otherwise go unnoticed for too long.

Typical service focus

  • Surface cleaning for dust and grime
  • Visual checks during maintenance visits
  • Bird netting and rooftop protection support
  • Recurring AMC-style planning where needed
