About SolarCare

SolarCare focuses on practical solar panel cleaning and maintenance support for rooftop systems in Lucknow, Kanpur, Unnao, and nearby areas. The aim is simple: help homes and businesses keep their systems cleaner, easier to manage, and less likely to lose output because of avoidable upkeep issues.

What SolarCare focuses on

  • Solar panel cleaning for homes and commercial rooftops
  • Maintenance visits with basic inspection support
  • Repair and inverter check assistance for common issues
  • Bird netting and related rooftop protection support
  • Recurring AMC-style service planning for larger or dust-prone sites

Who this site is built for

This website is built for homeowners, housing societies, schools, offices, and commercial operators who want clear service information before making a booking request. It explains the kind of support available, the areas served, and how customers can raise a cleaning, maintenance, or inspection request.

Why maintenance matters

Solar performance can decline gradually when dust, bird activity, blocked drainage, or repeated rooftop issues are ignored. Good maintenance is not only about washing panels. It also creates a regular opportunity to notice visible wear, access problems, bird-related damage, or service needs before they turn into larger disruptions.
