Why is solar panel cleaning important?

Dust and dirt can block sunlight and affect the output of solar panels. Cleaning helps maintain proper exposure and keeps the system in better visible condition.

How often should panels be cleaned?

This depends on location and dust level, but many systems benefit from regular cleaning every few weeks, especially in dusty or bird-prone areas.

Do you provide regular maintenance plans?

Yes, IMSOLARCARE offers AMC options for customers who want repeated service visits and more consistent upkeep.
