FAQ

Frequently asked questions

These are the most common questions customers ask before booking solar panel cleaning or AMC support.

How often should solar panels be cleaned?

In dusty areas, cleaning every 15 to 30 days is usually helpful. Frequency depends on dust, pollution, and bird activity.

Does cleaning improve solar output?

Yes. Dirty panels may produce less electricity. Regular cleaning helps maintain better performance.

Do you provide AMC plans?

Yes, IMSOLARCARE provides affordable annual maintenance plans for regular service visits.
