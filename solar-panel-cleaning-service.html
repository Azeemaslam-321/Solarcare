Why cleaning matters

Dirty panels can reduce visible exposure to sunlight

Dust, pollution haze, bird waste, and hard water marks build up slowly. Many rooftop owners notice the dirt only when it becomes obvious, but performance can be affected earlier. Regular cleaning keeps the system in better visible condition and supports more dependable output.

Who it is for

Residential and commercial rooftops

This service is suitable for home systems, office rooftops, schools, shops, apartment properties, and other solar installations that need routine or first-time cleaning support.

Common cleaning triggers

  • Visible dust layer on panel glass
  • Bird droppings or nesting around the system
  • Long gap since the last cleaning visit
  • Rooftops near roads, open plots, or construction areas
